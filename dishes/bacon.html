BACON WRAPPED TURKEY

Description

Turkey wrapped in bacon is a simple and unique Thanksgiving meal! Turkey should be cooked for 13 minutes per pound.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup dried sage
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 1(16 pound) whole turkey, neck and giblets remove
  • 2(12 ounce)packages bacon
  • 2 cups water

Steps in Making Bacon Wrapped Turkey

  1. Step 1:

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees C). Insert a rack for roasting into a big baking dish.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Sprinkle sage and garlic powder over entire turkey. Completely wrap turkey in bacon. Place the turkey on the prepared rack in the baking dish. Water is poured into the plate.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Bake the turkey in a preheated oven, basting every 45 minutes with the juices in the baking dish, for 3 1/2 to 4 hours, or until the meat is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear. A thermometer put into the thickest portion of the thigh should register at least 165 degrees Fahrenheit (74 degrees C).
