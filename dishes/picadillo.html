AUTHENTIC MEXICAN PICADILLO

Description

Picadillo is a prominent dish in the cuisines of South America. It is comprised of lean ground beef (or ground turkey, if desired), tomatoes, potatoes, and other ingredients. This original dish will bring Mexican flavors to your table.

Ingredients

  • 6 tomatoes
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 onion, halved
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 serrano pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 (7.75 ounce) can Mexican-style hot tomato sauce
  • 3 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 medium carrots, peeled and cubed
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons chicken bouillon granules
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps in Making Authentic Mexican Picadillo

  1. Step 1:

    2. Bring to a boil six whole tomatoes in a small pot. Transfer to a blender. Add onion halves and a whole garlic clove to 3/4 cup water. Blend till creamy.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Large skillet with oil heated over medium-high heat. Add finely minced onion and serrano pepper. Cook onion till translucent. Add minced garlic and simmer for 1 minute, or until fragrant. Add chopped tomato and simmer for an additional minute Add ground beef and brown for approximately 8 minutes.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Pour tomato sauce from a can into the beef mixture. Prepare for 2 minutes. Incorporate potatoes and carrots. Add the pureed tomatoes, jalapeno, chicken bouillon, cumin, and bay leaf after two minutes of cooking. Salt and pepper are used as seasonings. Mix thoroughly, decrease heat, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until the majority of the liquid has been absorbed. Let sit for 5 minutes before serving.
